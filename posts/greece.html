Greece Trip

August 1, 2018

My trip to Greece

24th of June Landed in Athens at 10am after a 20+ hour flight - very tired. Visited the Acropolis and the surrounding sites. Also visited theAcropolis Museum. Flew to Santorini at 9pm.


25th of June Explored Santorini. Hung out in the main town Fira. Walked from Fira to Oia (North of the island). Got sunburnt. Lots of mistreated donkeys on the way to the old port. Ride back up the hill. 26th of June Went southward to the "red beach". Overnight ferry to Naxos. 27th of June Explored Naxos island. 28th of June Choices were either hire a scooter or a quad bike to explore Naxos. I chose the guided tour bus. The tour guide (and bus driver were very good. Temple of Demeter. 29th of June Another bus tour but a different route. 30th of June Ferry back to Athens. Bus to Nafplio. Views from the Palmidi Tower. 1st of July Hired a bicycle and rode from one beach to another. Back to where I started the day.
